Services

How We Can Help

Whether you're new to technology or just need a refresher, we're here to help at your pace.

Smartphones & Tablets

Setting up devices, managing contacts, and using apps for photos and video calls with family.

Computers & Email

Email basics, staying safe online, organizing files, and troubleshooting common issues.

Smart TVs & Streaming

Connecting to Wi‑Fi, using Netflix and other services, and remote control basics.

Video Calls & Staying Connected

Zoom, FaceTime, and other video apps so you can see and talk with family and friends.

Photos & Social Media

Sharing photos, Facebook basics, and staying in touch with loved ones online.

Safety & Security

Recognizing scams, strong passwords, and protecting your privacy online.
